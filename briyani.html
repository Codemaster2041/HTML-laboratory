PREPARATION OF BRIYANI

REQUIRED INGRIDIENTS:

Ingredients Quantity
Basmati Rice 2 cups
Chicken (with bones) 500g
Onion 2 (sliced)
Tomato 2 (chopped)
Yogurt 1 cup
Ginger Garlic Paste 2 tbsp
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Red Chili Powder 1 tsp
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tsp
Cumin Powder 1/2 tsp
Coriander Powder 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste
Ghee 3 tbsp
Water 4 cups



PROCEDURE:

  1. Wash 2 cups of basmati rice in water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a large pot on medium heat and add 3 tablespoons of ghee. Once the ghee has melted, add 2 sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add 2 chopped tomatoes to the pot and fry for 2-3 minutes until the tomatoes have softened.
  4. Add 500g of chicken (with bones) to the pot and fry for 5-7 minutes until the chicken is lightly browned.
  5. Add 2 tablespoons of ginger garlic paste, 1 teaspoon of garam masala, 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon of cumin powder, 1/2 teaspoon of coriander powder, and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add 1 cup of yogurt to the pot and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Gently mix the rice and chicken mixture together.
  8. Add 4 cups of water to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and cook for 20-25 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked and the water has been absorbed.
  9. Once the biryani is cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  10. Fluff the biryani with a fork and serve hot.
